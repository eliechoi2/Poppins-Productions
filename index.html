ABOUT US

HOW WE GOT HERE

Founded in 2015, Poppins Productions and brand portfolio Poppins Extensions Plus introduces a entertainment/business management and consultancy services located in the Midwest where we focus in the development of individuals from within the business and entertainment industries to take care of their careers and to become better leaders. We are proud to provide a unique development program which integrates our business communications and financial literacy into our teachings.

Our services are available to all clients regardless of skill level from beginners to elite clients such as Comedian Charles Walden, a popular comedian who previously starred in the hit television shows, “BET Comic View” and “Def Comedy Jam”, Comedian Famo who starred in the movie, “Not Another Black Movie”, Manasseh who hit song, “Inches”, was featured on Tyler’s Perry Sista’s.

We are committed to black excellence through philanthropy on a national and local level, creating a robust internship program, and ability to create different brand portfolio’s to provide entertainers with a new platform to showcase their talents with Poppins Urban Podcast. We are excited for the future and always looking for ways to expand and grow.
